Core Values

We seek to be characterized by our passion, integrity, entrepreneurialism and our innovative spirit, as we continually strive to fulfill our vision. These values guide us in all that we do and are the bedrock upon which our organization is built.

  1. We focus on the needs of our students, customers and stakeholders and as well as responding to the changing needs of our communities in a timely manner through our innovative spirit.
  2. We promote creativity, innovation, and the development of individuals, institutions and corporate organizations by the use of IT.
  3. We have a compelling desire to improve and to win in the marketplace.
  4. We accept personal accountability to meet the business needs, improve our systems, and help others improve their effectiveness through creativity, and allowing the freedom of thought and creative expression.
  5. We operate with passion, transparency, commitment and integrity.
  6. We establish partnership with stakeholders in skills, concepts, and knowledge generation and application in order to ensure that the quality of programmes/courses offered meets both local and global standards.
  7. We recognize that profitability is essential to our future success.

QUALITY POLICY AND PROFESSIONALISM

GCI quality and professionalism are key in ensuring the long-term sustainability of business and the institution. The following quality objectives described below are a cornerstone in ensuring high levels of customer satisfaction by maintaining academic and operational excellence in the delivery of our services. It is therefore our commitment to: .

  1. Develop the understanding that we will never compromise with quality.
  2. Deliver relevant and world-class courses and services to meet our customers' needs.
  3. Actively adhere to industry and customer needs and expectations.
  4. Ensure effective training of staffs in a bid to improve skills and expertise.
  5. Establish and maintain a level of quality which enhances the institution's reputation with clients, other organizations and our community.
  6. Protect GCI's intellectual property and know-how
  7. Be committed to continuous improvement to ensure that we remain competent in offering approved IT programmes and all other related services to sectors and location designated by clients.
